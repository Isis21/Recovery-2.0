What is a Support

Going into this I didn't know what a support actually was.
I thought a support was someone who would take care of me.
Would watch over me, would provide for me, give me exactly what I wanted
When I Wanted it.

Don't get me wrong, this program isn't about telling you how to feel, but for me and many others,
this is what we experienced. You have to find your own way through the mud that is acting out.
I can't carry you, but I can support you.
Now what exactly is a support, a healthy support?

For me it's having an ear and advice, someone to help take my mind off of the suffering that i'm feeling.
Sure I have to do this myself and i'm learning to do so, but for right now, it's better than watching porn
or WORSE

This support would remind the other participant that there is light at the end of the tunnel.
That life doesn't end when they relapse. That life is filled with more splendor than the addiction
could ever offer. I will go over some of these things in my Lines section in more detail.
They would also remind them that the 12 steps of recovery are a great resource,
one of the primary resources to a successful recovery.
