The Pledge

My pledge is to help you become a better you. I will not give up on you, until you give up on you. Helping you helps me stay clean.
I pledge to be a temporary sponsor, meaning that I will be your sponsor, until you find
a more permenant one. I won't skip out on any of the deeds that a primary sponsor would
but, the big but here is that I don't have the clean time nor do I have the experience
with the steps. What I can give though is my experience, an ear and my wisdom.
