Step 2

Came to believe that a power greater than ourselves could restore us to sanity.

Before you run for the hills there is many ways of looking at this higher power thing.
For starters a simple definition is something larger than yourself that can sustain you
in your recovery. This thing has to be healthy and constantly keep you moving forward.
At the very least it has to keep you maintaining healthy habits if not progressing towards
something. This larger thing can be traditional God, Allah, etc, etc. I'm not a huge fan
but, I will admit that believing in a traditional God works in this program if you can wrap
your head around the concepts and give yourself fully. Option 2 is the option I took.
It requires you to believe in one thing, that a greater sense of yourself exist and is attainable.
Option 3 is the group.You can look at the meetings as maintaining your honesty
and accountability towards your recovery.
