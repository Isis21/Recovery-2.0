Lines

What is a Top Line and what is their purpose? What is some examples of Top lines?
What is a Bottom line and a Middle Circle (Slippery Slope)? I will discuss these next.

Bottom-Line

Bottom Line behaviors are the behaviors that you A: don't want to do, B: others don't want you to do and/or
C:Will get/ is hurt you or kill you. These behaviors can range from pornography, prostitutes, drinking,
eating excessivly, gambling, recreational drugs, prescription drugs, etc. The list can go on really
Hell, you can be addicted to work. Think of it like this, if it harms you and your life is unmanageable,
your an addict. It just depends if you can accept it yet (Step 1).

Middle Circle

Middle Circle behaviors are the behaviors that can get you into trouble or lead to trouble.
These vary from person to person in the rooms. In SA, some can masturbate while staying sober, others can't.
In AA, some can go near a bar or beer distributer and be ok, others will go in and get some booze.
These middle circle behaviors are like I put it, a slippery slope. Instead of pushing your limits
on your bottom lines; why not push the limits on your top lines?

Top-Line

Top Line behaviors are the behaviors that keep you sober and healthy. These not just keep you closer
to your higher power, but keep you closer to yourself. You can discover so much about yourself if
just give in and let life be life, accept you don't have any control over certain things, like death
Paying rent, losing a significant other, paying taxes and accept the control you do have. Use the
descretion your higher power gave you to make a sound decision and CHOOSE to be happy.
