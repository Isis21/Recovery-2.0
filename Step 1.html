Step 1

We Admited we were powerless over our addiction, that our lives had become unmanageable

This took me quite a while to work through. I didn't want to admit that I had a problem
hell, everyone else was the problem. Well, that was the problem... If I can give anyone some
advice on this to help them through this step I would say be honest with yourself.
Can you look in the mirror and honestly say your safe? That your health isn't at risk?
That your relationships aren't at risk? That your FUTURE isn't at risk?
Honesty is the core of this program, with yourself, with others and with your higher power.
