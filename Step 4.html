Step 4

Made a searching and fearless inventory of ourselves

This is where the work begins.You will work on your fears, your resentments, your life history
All to give it your higher power to handle. This process is challenging because you may remember
the big things, such as abusive relationship, but won't remember dropping a battery on your foot
just yesterday. You will be adding to the list as time goes on and that's ok. You will remember things
things that you locked away deep in your psyche, but you will have support through the group,
members and whatever sponsor you take up. The big question here is "why do it at all"? Why bring
up all this baggage? Well, all this baggage has been haunting us for years if not longer. Pulling
our strings from the sidelines. I can only speak for myself here, but I can tell you this, when I hear
others speak about their experiences, I'm like, damn man, I went through that as well and i'm
currently going through that. For example: My dad left at 12 when he found out that I was
mentally challenged (bipolar). Later that would give me abandonment issues that would make
me very dependent on my mother and later females for my emotional and physical support.
I would not feel comfortable in my body and would pickup and continue a porn habit along
with objectification of women and getting sex workers. See how one little event can manipulate
your whole life in a horrible direction? See how the baggage is with you if you don't course correct?
